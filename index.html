üçã Lemon Parmesan Risotto

Description

Serves 2. Nothing is more comforting than a warm bowl of cheesy risotto after a long day... add fresh lemon for an energizing kick!

Ingredients

  • 3 cloves of garlic (peeled)
  • 2 shallots
  • 1 cup arborio rice
  • 1 carton chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup parmesan (grated)
  • 1 lemon

Steps

  1. Chop garlic & shallots.
  2. In a large pot, pour a thin layer of olive oil. Cook garlic & shallots on medium heat until lightly browned.
  3. Add the uncooked rice and drizzle some additional oil on top. Cook for a few minutes, stirring consistently until the rice looks more clear and toasty.
  4. Use a ladle to add small portions of the chicken stock, enough to cover the rice. Stir until the rice has soaked up the liquid. Repeat until the rice is fully cooked through (approx. 20-30 minutes).
  5. Turn off the heat. Stir in the grated parmesan, then squeeze fresh lemon juice to finish.

ü•´ Easy Pasta Pomodoro

Description

Serves 2. Easy, quick, and delicious pomodoro sauce made from canned peeled tomatoes ‚Äì imported from Italy will taste the most fresh!

Ingredients

  • 1 can peeled tomatoes
  • 3 cloves garlic (peeled)
  • 1 yellow onion
  • Penne, gemelli, or any other pasta shape (cook separately)

Steps

  1. Chop garlic & onion.
  2. In a large pot, pour a thin layer of olive oil. Cook garlic & onion on medium heat until lightly browned.
  3. Add in the peeled tomatoes. Cook on medium-low heat until the tomatoes are soft enough to crush with the spoon.
  4. Continue to cook and stir the sauce, adding salt to taste. Let the sauce simmer uncovered (at least 20 minutes) while you cook the pasta.

üçù Spaghetti & Meatballs

Description

Serves 2. The best recipe for meatballs ever.

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/3 cup parmesan (grated)
  • 1 clove garlic (minced)
  • 1-2 cups store-bought tomato sauce
  • Spaghetti or any other pasta shape (cook separately)

Steps

  1. Crack the egg in a large bowl and whisk with a fork.
  2. Mix in the ground beef with your hands. Wear disposable gloves...
  3. Combine milk and breadcrumbs a liquid measuring cup, then add to the beef and mix.
  4. Combine the grated parmesan with the beef mixture, adding garlic powder and onion powder if desired.
  5. Form the beef mixture into small meatballs.
  6. In a large nonstick pan, pour in a thick layer of olive oil and heat on medium-low.
  7. Cook the meatballs in the pan until browned (approx. 10 minutes).
  8. In a large pot, cook the minced garlic in a thin layer of olive oil on medium heat until lightly browned.
  9. Add tomato sauce to the large pot and simmer with the garlic. Add salt to taste.
  10. Transfer the meatballs to the sauce in the large pot, stir, cover, and simmer on low heat (approx. 20-30 minutes).
