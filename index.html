üçã Lemon Parmesan Risotto

Description

Nothing is more comforting than a warm bowl of cheesy risotto after a long day... add lemon for an energizing kick!

Ingredients

  • 1 lemon
  • 2 shallots
  • 3 cloves of garlic (peeled)
  • 1 cup arborio rice
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup parmesan (grated)

Steps

  1. Chop garlic & shallots.
  2. In a large pot, pour a thin layer of olive oil. Cook garlic & shallots on medium heat until lightly browned.
  3. Add the uncooked rice with some additional oil and cook for a few minutes.
  4. Add small portions of chicken stock with a ladle, stirring consistently until cooked through, approx. 20 min.
  5. Finish with parmesan and lemon juice!
